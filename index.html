About me

Hi there, My name is Juan Leal. I'm a UX/UI designer who loves to craft user centered digital products. I like to ask a lot of questions to really understand the problems and goals of the users, and then apply my creativity with a systematic approach to address those goals and bring great product experiences to their life.

I'm also passionate with everything design, storytelling and exploring unfamiliar territories and fields to master both my problem-solving skills and visual craft.

Right now I'm open to opportunities to work with (and learn from) cool teams, so hit me up if you want to chat.
